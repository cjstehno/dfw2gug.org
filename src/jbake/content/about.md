The Dallas/Fort Worth Groovy & Grails user group launched on June 2<sup>nd</sup> 2010 and has been meeting ever since. 

You can learn more about our group from the meetings archive, announcements on our [Google Group](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/dfw2gug), or follow us on twitter [@dfw2gug](https://twitter.com/dfw2gug) for the latest info.

The group meets on the 1<sup>st</sup> Wednesday of every month, with social/food at 6:30pm while the meeting content starts at 7:00pm. 

The meetings take place at the [Improving Enterprises](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Improving+Enterprises,+Inc./@32.977034,-96.826953,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m2!3m1!1s0x864c214428760391:0x93e3cd120aeda77a) office on the Dallas North Tollway.

    16633 Dallas Parkway #100
    Addison, TX 75001
