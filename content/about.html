title=About date=2015-02-11 type=page status=published ~~~~~~ The Dallas/Fort Worth Groovy & Grails user group launched on June 2nd 2010 and has been meeting ever since. You can learn more about our group from the meetings archive, announcements on our Google Group, or follow us on twitter @dfw2gug for the latest info. The group meets on the 1st Wednesday of every month, with social/food at 6:30pm while the meeting content starts at 7:00pm. The meetings take place at the Improving Enterprises office on the Dallas North Tollway. 16633 Dallas Parkway #100 Addison, TX 75001
