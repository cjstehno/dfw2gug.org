A user group focused on expanding the breadth and depth of Groovy and Grails development skills with our members who live and work in the DFW area. The group launched on June 2nd 2010 and have been meeting ever since. You can learn more about our group from the meetings archive, announcements on our Google Group, or follow us on twitter @dfw2gug for the latest info. The group meets on the 1st Wednesday of every month, with social / food at 6:30pm; the meeting content starts at 7:00pm. We meet at the Improving Enterprises office on the Dallas North Tollway.
