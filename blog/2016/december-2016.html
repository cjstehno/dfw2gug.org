Gradle Plugins 101

07 December 2016 - Christopher J Stehno

Abstract

Last month we had an overview of Gradle. This month we delve deeper in and talk about how and why to develop Gradle plugins by developing a simple one during the meeting.

If you missed last month's meeting you can read a great blog post from Chris that covers the same Intro to Gradle material he covered in the meeting.

Bio

Christopher J. Stehno is a Sr Engineer at NetScout in Plano, TX. He has been a Java developer and Web Developer for more than 16 years and a Groovy developer for six years. In his free time he enjoys hacking on his personal projects, exploring the DFW area with his wife, reading pulpy science fiction novels and hiking.
