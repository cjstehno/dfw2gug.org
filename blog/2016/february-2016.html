Ratpapck, what is it and why do you care?

03 February 2016 - Steve Good

Abstract

Ratpack, what is it and why do you care?

During this talk, Steve will be covering what makes Ratpack different from other application frameworks, how to consume and generate streams of data through the framework and general non-blocking I/O principals (within the context of the framework).

Bio

Steve is a solutions architect at BNSF Railway focused primarily on a multi-tier customer facing application written in Grails and Spring Boot. He spends most of his spare time experimenting with new languages and technologies.
